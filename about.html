About Me

_

Hi, I'm Nweze Augustine, but you can call me Austine Young. I'm a creative and detail-oriented graphics designer with a passion for crafting visually stunning designs that tell a story. By day, I'm a student in the Department of Electronics and Computer Engineering Nnamdi Azikiwe University, but by night, I'm a designer bringing ideas to life.

My Journey

As a student in a technical field, I've always been fascinated by the intersection of art and technology. I've honed my skills in graphics design through online courses, tutorials, and personal projects. My passion for design has led me to explore various styles and mediums, from digital illustrations to branding and UI/UX design.

My Approach

I'm a problem-solver at heart, and I approach each design project with a critical eye. I believe that good design should be both functional and beautiful, and I strive to create work that is both visually stunning and effective in communicating its message.

What I'm Looking For

I'm excited to collaborate with clients who share my passion for innovative design and storytelling. Whether you're looking for a logo, branding package, or digital illustration, I'm here to help bring your vision to life.
